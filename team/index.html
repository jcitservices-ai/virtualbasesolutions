Behind Virtual βase Solutions are two founders with one shared vision: helping businesses scale with the right people, the right systems, and the right execution.

Ma. Lorena “Yen” Belonio

Co-Founder

Yen Belonio built her career by understanding how businesses actually run. For more than a decade, she has worked behind the scenes of growing organizations across sales, legal support, and recruitment, where execution matters more than theory and results matter more than promises.

She has supported U.S. law firms and service-based businesses through case support, demand preparation, recruitment, and operations management. That experience shaped a clear point of view: many businesses do not struggle because of lack of opportunity, they struggle because of inefficiency, misalignment, and people placed in the wrong roles.

That insight became the foundation of Virtual βase Solutions. Yen focuses on delivering reliable, high-performing remote talent that understands the workflow, takes ownership, and creates measurable impact from day one.

Her Approach

  • Hire for impact, not just availability.
  • Build systems that scale instead of breaking under pressure.
  • Operate lean without sacrificing quality.

What She’s Building

Yen’s vision is to prove that scaling a business is not about adding more people. It is about placing the right people in the right roles with the right structure. She remains closely involved in client strategy and team development so every partnership is built for long-term success.

Lara Jane Gulane

Co-Founder

Lara’s journey has always been rooted in understanding what truly drives business growth. Long before building teams at scale, she was working across sales, recruiting, and business development, where consistency, performance, and results were never optional.

Over the years, Lara has helped companies across SaaS, B2B, and high-growth industries build stronger pipelines, close more opportunities, and hire the kind of talent that actually moves the needle. From leading outbound sales efforts and SDR teams to building strategic partnerships and recruiting top-tier sales professionals, she has seen what separates companies that grow from those that stall.

That perspective led her to co-found Virtual βase Solutions with a focus on predictable growth through structured execution and high-performing teams. Her strength is aligning pipeline generation and talent acquisition into one cohesive system built for clarity and long-term results.

Her Approach

  • Build pipelines that convert, not just fill dashboards.
  • Hire people who perform, not just people who meet requirements.
  • Create systems that drive consistency, not chaos.

What She’s Building

Lara’s vision is to help businesses move from inconsistent growth to repeatable success powered by the right people, the right systems, and the right strategy. She stays hands-on with each partnership to ensure every team and process is built to create long-term impact.
